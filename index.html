ABOUT ME

My name is Earl Xavier Espanta, and I am a UX/UI Designer based in the Washington DC Metropolitan Area. Prior to being based in the DC area, I lived in Los Angeles, CA for about 5 years pursuing my studies in the City of Dreams. Currently holding a Masters Degree in Marketing, I wanted to find ways of taking my knowledge of consumer behavior into the budding digital world, and this led me to pursue UX Design and Research.

I am passionate about the creative world in all aspects; when I am not designing, you can find me in dance classes or at a photoshoot. I enjoy the performing arts and connecting with other individuals who are in the same field!

Traveling and learning about new cultures has shaped me into the person I am today, ask me about the places I’ve been and cities I’ve explored. Who knows, I may be in your city next! ☺️
